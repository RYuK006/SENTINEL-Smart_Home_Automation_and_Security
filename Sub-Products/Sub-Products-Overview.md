# Sentinel Sub-Products

The Sentinel ecosystem extends beyond the core home hub through a suite of integrated sub-products. Each device is designed to solve a specific physical security or safety challenge while operating securely within Sentinel's localized, edge-AI architecture.

## The Sub-Product Suite

*   [Sentinel Band](Sentinel-Band.md): The primary wearable identity token, panic button, and local vitals monitor.
*   [Sentinel Cradle](Sentinel-Cradle.md): Contactless infant vitals and environment monitor using 60GHz mmWave radar.
*   [Sentinel Trace](Sentinel-Trace.md): Covert, ultra-low power LTE-M/GPS tracker for highly mobile assets and vulnerable individuals.
*   [Sentinel Air](Sentinel-Air.md): Autonomous, Nano-category perimeter response drone for elevated visual verification.
*   [Sentinel Swarm](Sentinel-Swarm.md): Decentralized, solar-powered LoRa mesh nodes for stretching perception across vast properties.
*   [Sentinel Whisper](Sentinel-Whisper.md): Covert acoustic threat detector and ultrasonic microphone jammer for extreme privacy enforcement.
*   [Sentinel Rover](Sentinel-Rover.md): Autonomous indoor ground patrol robot for dynamic visual investigation of anomalies.

Each sub-product feeds directly into the Perceive, Verify, Contain, Escalate, and Document layers of the primary Sentinel Hub.
