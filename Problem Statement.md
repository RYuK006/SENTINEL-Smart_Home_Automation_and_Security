# Problem Statement

Home security systems sit at two broken extremes. Commercial systems (ADT, SimpliSafe) are reliable but prohibitively expensive with recurring fees, and they route every emergency through a human call center, slowing response times. DIY systems (Arduino + PIR) are cheap but "dumb": they alert on literally anything that moves, generate massive false alarms, and take zero physical action. Neither category provides a unified system for tracking child safety, monitoring elderly vitals, containing a physical threat, or communicating with emergency services when Wi-Fi and power are cut.
