# Existing Solutions and Their Limitations

*   **Commercial Alarms:** Expensive, closed, reactive, and reliant on human dispatchers. 
*   **DIY Security Kits:** Pure rule-based logic (motion = buzzer). Cannot distinguish a human from a cat or a curtain.
*   **Smart Cameras (Ring, Nest):** Reactive (notify but don’t act). Rely on the cloud (privacy concerns) and do not physically track or contain threats.
*   **Satellite SOS & Wearables (Apple, Life360):** Standalone features disconnected from the household infrastructure.
