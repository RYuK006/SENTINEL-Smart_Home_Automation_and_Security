# Domestic Help Access Flow

When the maid arrives, they scan their fingerprint on [Sentinel Access](../Core-Components/Sentinel%20Access.md). The system unlocks the kitchen and utility doors but keeps the master bedroom and [Sentinel Safe](../Core-Components/Sentinel%20Safe.md) locked. The owner receives a log of the entry and exit times.
