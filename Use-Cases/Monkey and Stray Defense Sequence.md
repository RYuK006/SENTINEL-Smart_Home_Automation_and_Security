# Monkey and Stray Defense Sequence

If monkeys or stray dogs breach the compound, the perimeter camera detects them. [Sentinel Sonic](../Core-Components/Sentinel%20Sonic.md) emits a painful (but harmless) high-frequency pitch. If they proceed to the lawn, [Sentinel Flora](../Core-Components/Sentinel%20Flora.md) activates the sprinklers to actively chase them away.
