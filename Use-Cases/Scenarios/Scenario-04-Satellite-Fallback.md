**Trigger**: Wi-Fi router destroyed by intruder
**Layers Activated**: Perceive, Escalate

## Narrative

This scenario details the exact response when Wi-Fi router destroyed by intruder occurs. As the raw telemetry—spanning acoustic signatures, thermal movement, and visual frames—floods the primary processing hub, the risk-scoring engine rapidly evaluates the context against historical baselines and current environmental parameters. Upon verifying the threat with absolute mathematical certainty, the system seamlessly transitions into its containment phase, executing physical deterrents and securing critical entry points to isolate the anomaly. Simultaneously, it prepares the escalation ladder, evaluating whether to contact the primary homeowner or proceed directly to an automated emergency dispatch. Throughout this entire ordeal, every millisecond of sensor data, AI inference confidence scores, and mechanical actuation logs are cryptographically hashed and compiled into a secure evidence package, ensuring that a tamper-proof chain of custody is maintained from the moment of detection until the final resolution of the incident.
