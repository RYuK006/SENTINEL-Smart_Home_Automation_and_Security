# Confidential Meeting Protection

When the homeowner needs a private conversation in the home office, they activate this mode. [Sentinel Whisper](../Core-Components/Sentinel%20Whisper.md) engages to jam all potential recording devices, and [Sentinel Shield](../Core-Components/Sentinel%20Shield.md) closes the smart blinds to prevent visual snooping or laser microphone eavesdropping.
