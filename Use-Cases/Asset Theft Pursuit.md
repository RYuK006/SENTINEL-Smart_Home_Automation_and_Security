# Asset Theft Pursuit

If a car or bicycle is stolen at 3 AM, [Sentinel Trace](../Core-Components/Sentinel%20Trace.md) detects the movement, wakes up, and alerts the hub. The hub instantly flashes the [Sentinel Beacon](../Core-Components/Sentinel%20Beacon.md), turns on exterior lights, and pushes live GPS coordinates to the owner and the police.
