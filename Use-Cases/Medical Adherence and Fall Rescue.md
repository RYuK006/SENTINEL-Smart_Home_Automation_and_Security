# Medical Adherence and Fall Rescue

If the elderly resident misses their medication window on the [Sentinel Med-Box](../Core-Components/Sentinel%20Med-Box.md), it alerts them. If shortly after, they suffer a fall detected by the [Sentinel Band](../Core-Components/Sentinel%20Band.md) and camera, the system skips the warning phase and instantly calls for an ambulance via [AI Voice Agent Dispatch](../Features/AI%20Voice%20Agent%20Dispatch.md).
