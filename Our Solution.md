# Our Solution: Sentinel

Sentinel is a unified household safety operating layer. It uses [[Multi-Sensor Fusion]] and [[On-Device Classification]] to *verify* threats before acting. Instead of a binary alarm, it follows a graduated escalation ladder: **Perceive → Verify → Contain → Escalate → Document**. It seamlessly integrates home security, family safety, elderly care, and energy automation into a single, closed, and highly secure ecosystem.
