# Our Solution: Sentinel

Sentinel is a unified household safety operating layer. It uses [Multi-Sensor Fusion](Features/Multi-Sensor%20Fusion.md) and [On-Device Classification](Features/On-Device%20Classification.md) to *verify* threats before acting. Instead of a binary alarm, it follows a graduated escalation ladder: **Perceive → Verify → Contain → Escalate → Document**. It seamlessly integrates home security, family safety, elderly care, and energy automation into a single, closed, and highly secure ecosystem.
