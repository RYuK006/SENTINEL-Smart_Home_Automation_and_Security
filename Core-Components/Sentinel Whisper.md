# Sentinel Whisper

A covert ultrasonic microphone jammer designed to protect your privacy during sensitive conversations in home offices or meeting rooms. It emits sweeping ultrasonic frequencies that are completely inaudible to human ears but overload the diaphragms of microphones on smartphones, smart speakers, and hidden bugs. It renders any unauthorized audio recording useless by filling it with loud static noise.
