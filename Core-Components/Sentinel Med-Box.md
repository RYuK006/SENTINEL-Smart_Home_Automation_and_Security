# Sentinel Med-Box

A smart medication dispenser tied to the system’s vitals monitoring (via the [Sentinel Band](Sentinel%20Band.md)). It securely dispenses medication at the right times and tracks adherence. If medication is missed, it escalates an alert to family members before a medical emergency occurs.
