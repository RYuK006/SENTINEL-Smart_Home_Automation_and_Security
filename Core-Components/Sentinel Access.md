# Sentinel Access

A dedicated biometric/RFID pad for the back or side gate, designed for domestic helpers, cooks, and regular vendors. It logs entry and exit times, restricts access to only specific zones (e.g., kitchen and balcony), and alerts the homeowner. You can generate temporary time-bound codes for maintenance workers.
