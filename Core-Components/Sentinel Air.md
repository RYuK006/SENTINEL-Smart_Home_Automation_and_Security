# Sentinel Air

Sentinel Air is the autonomous perimeter response drone designed as an advanced/future-scope escalation tier for the Sentinel system.

## Key Concepts
*   **Autonomous Perimeter Response:** Deploys when high-severity outdoor events occur. 
*   **Elevated Wide Shots:** Launches, hovers, and captures an elevated wide shot of the perimeter to provide better context than fixed cameras.
*   **Target Pursuit (Future Scope):** Visual tracking of a moving person from the drone to keep them in frame and get identification.
*   **Multi-Modal Handoff:** Integrates with the central hub's [[Risk Scoring Engine]] and [[Multi-Camera Handoff]] to track intruders across zones.

*Note: For the college prototype, this is framed as a fixed perimeter version or a simple launch-and-hover demo due to drone regulations and autonomous pursuit complexity.*
