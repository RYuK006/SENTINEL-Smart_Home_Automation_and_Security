# Sentinel Shield

Smart, motorized window blinds/shutters made of reinforced material. They serve a dual purpose: closing automatically for energy efficiency during heatwaves ([Weather-Reactive Prep](../Use-Cases/Weather-Reactive%20Prep.md)), and slamming shut instantly if a perimeter breach is detected, physically hardening the house against intrusion.
