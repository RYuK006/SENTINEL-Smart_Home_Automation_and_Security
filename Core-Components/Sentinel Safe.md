# Sentinel Safe

A biometric indoor safe for valuables, documents, or firearms. It unlocks via the [Sentinel Band](Sentinel%20Band.md)’s proximity + fingerprint. If it detects movement (someone trying to carry it away) or unauthorized tampering, it immediately escalates a high-severity alert to the hub.
