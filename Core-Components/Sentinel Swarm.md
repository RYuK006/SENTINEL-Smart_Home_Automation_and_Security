# Sentinel Swarm

A smart automated mosquito and vector control vaporizer. It operates intelligently by depending directly on data from the [[Sentinel Grill]]. If the Grill detects that windows are open during dusk or high-risk times, Sentinel Swarm automatically activates to protect the household from dengue/malaria vectors. If the grills are closed, it stays off to conserve chemical refills.
