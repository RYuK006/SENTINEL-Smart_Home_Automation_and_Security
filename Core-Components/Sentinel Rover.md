# Sentinel Rover

A small, low-profile autonomous ground robot. If motion is detected in a blind spot or a glass-break sensor trips ([Acoustic Event Detection](../Features/Acoustic%20Event%20Detection.md)), the Rover navigates to that specific room to provide a live, mobile camera feed of the incident without risking human confrontation.
