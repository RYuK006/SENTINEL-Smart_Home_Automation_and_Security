# Sentinel Valve

A motorized smart valve that retrofits directly over the home’s main water or gas line. It is hardwired to the home's local mesh network. If the hub's AI predicts a dangerous gas leak via [Predictive Gas-Leak Detection](../Features/Predictive%20Gas-Leak%20Detection.md) or a water leak sensor is tripped, the Sentinel Valve physically shuts off the supply in seconds, preventing disaster.
