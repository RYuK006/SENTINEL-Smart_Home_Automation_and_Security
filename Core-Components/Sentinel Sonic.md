# Sentinel Sonic

A specialized deterrent for stray animals (monkeys, dogs, cows). When the on-device AI camera detects an animal (distinguishing them from humans or pets via [On-Device Classification](../Features/On-Device%20Classification.md)), Sentinel Sonic triggers high-frequency ultrasonic sounds and strobe lights to harmlessly drive them away without causing neighborhood noise disturbance.
